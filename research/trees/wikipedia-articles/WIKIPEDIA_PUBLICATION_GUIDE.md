## üöÄ Step-by-Step Publication Instructions

### Step 1: Create Wikipedia Account (If Needed)

1. Go to https://en.wikipedia.org/
2. Click "Create account" (top right)
3. Choose username (suggest: Your real name or "Sparks19923" as mentioned)
4. Complete registration
5. **IMPORTANT**: Wait 4 days before creating articles (Wikipedia's "autoconfirmed" requirement)

**Why the wait?** Wikipedia requires new accounts to be at least 4 days old and have made 10 edits before creating new articles. This prevents spam.

### Step 2: Make Your First 10 Edits (Required)

To become "autoconfirmed," you need 10 edits. Here's how:

**Easy first edits:**
1. Fix typos in existing articles (search for "typo" on Wikipedia)
2. Add citations to articles marked with "citation needed"
3. Improve formatting in stub articles
4. Add missing birth/death dates to biographical articles
5. Correct grammar errors

**Tips:**
- Make genuine improvements, not trivial changes
- Use edit summaries (explain what you changed)
- Follow Wikipedia's style guidelines

### Step 3: Upload to Your Sandbox

Once your account is 4 days old with 10+ edits:

1. Go to your sandbox: `https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/User:YourUsername/Sandbox`
2. Click "Edit source" (NOT "Edit")
3. **Copy-paste the entire contents** of one .wiki file
4. Click "Publish changes"
5. Review how it looks

**Start with Charles John Vincent** (strongest notability case).

### Step 4: Move from Sandbox to Main Wikipedia

#### Method A: Articles for Creation (Recommended for New Users)

1. At the top of your sandbox page, add this template:
   ```
   {{subst:AFC submission|ts=~~~~~}}
   ```
2. This submits your article for review by experienced editors
3. Wait 1-6 weeks for review (Wikipedia reviewers are volunteers)
4. Respond to any feedback and revise as needed

#### Method B: Direct Creation (For Experienced Users)

1. Search for the article title (e.g., "Charles John Vincent")
2. If no article exists, you'll see "Create this page"
3. Click "Create this page"
4. Copy-paste the wikitext from your .wiki file
5. Add edit summary: "Creating article on Victorian composer/organist"
6. Click "Publish page"

## üìñ Wikipedia Policies to Understand

### 1. Neutral Point of View (NPOV)
‚úÖ **Good**: "He founded St. Peter's Church in 1896"
‚ùå **Bad**: "He heroically founded St. Peter's Church despite opposition"

**Your articles**: Already written in neutral tone ‚úÖ

### 2. Verifiability (V)
‚úÖ **Good**: Every statement has a <ref> citation
‚ùå **Bad**: "He was a skilled mason" (no source)

**Your articles**: All key facts cited ‚úÖ

### 3. No Original Research (NOR)
‚úÖ **Good**: "The National Archives documents show..."
‚ùå **Bad**: "Based on the records, he probably felt..."

**Your articles**: No original research present ‚úÖ

### 4. Notability (N)
‚úÖ **Notable**: Subject has received significant coverage in reliable, independent sources
‚ùå **Not Notable**: Subject only mentioned in family trees or self-published sources

**Your articles**: Varying levels (see assessment above)

## üîß Common Wikipedia Editing Issues & Fixes

### Issue 1: "Citation Needed" Tags

**What it looks like**: {{citation needed}}
**Why it happens**: Reviewers want a source for a specific claim
**How to fix**: Add a <ref> tag with appropriate archive reference

### Issue 2: "Notability" Challenges

**What happens**: Reviewer may nominate article for deletion (AfD)
**How to respond**: Provide additional sources showing independent coverage
**Defense strategy**: Point to academic databases (LBS), government archives (NRHP), institutional records

### Issue 3: "Conflicts of Interest" (COI)

**Wikipedia concern**: Family members writing about relatives
**How to handle**:
- Disclose relationship: "Note: I am a descendant researching family history"
- Use only verifiable sources (no family stories)
- Avoid promotional language
- Focus on documented facts

**Your advantage**: You're citing archives, not family memories ‚úÖ

## üõ†Ô∏è Technical Notes

### Wikitext Formatting Used

| Code | Effect | Example |
|------|--------|---------|
| `'''Bold'''` | **Bold text** | `'''Charles John Vincent'''` |
| `''Italic''` | *Italic text* | `''The Young Phoenix''` |
| `[[Link]]` | Internal link | `[[University of Oxford]]` |
| `<ref></ref>` | Footnote citation | `<ref>National Archives T71/249</ref>` |
| `{{template}}` | Insert template | `{{Infobox person}}` |
| `==Heading==` | Section heading | `==Early life==` |

### Categories Explained

Each article has categories at the bottom like:
```
[[Category:1852 births]]
[[Category:English composers]]
```

These organize Wikipedia articles. Reviewers may add more categories or suggest changes.

## üìû What to Do If Articles Are Rejected

### Common Rejection Reasons

1. **"Notability not established"**
   - **Response**: Provide additional sources
   - **Action**: Search newspaper archives, university records, church archives

2. **"Lacks independent sources"**
   - **Response**: Emphasize that National Archives, UCL database, NRHP are independent
   - **Action**: Add newspaper articles, academic papers, or books mentioning the subject

3. **"Reads like a memorial"**
   - **Response**: Remove any laudatory language
   - **Action**: Revise to be more clinical/encyclopedic

4. **"Conflict of interest"**
   - **Response**: Acknowledge relationship, emphasize reliance on archives
   - **Action**: Request uninvolved editor assistance

### Getting Help

- **Wikipedia Help Desk**: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Help_desk
- **WikiProject Biography**: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Biography
- **WikiProject Christianity** (for clergy): https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Christianity
