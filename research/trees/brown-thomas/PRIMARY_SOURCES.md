## 1. Thomas J. Brown (1860-1951)

### Obituary

**Title:** THOMAS J. BROWN, RETIRED RANCHER, DIES AT 90

**Published:** The Fresno Bee, January 8, 1951

**Full Text:**
> Thomas J. Brown, 90, a resident of Fresno for the past 50 years, passed away yesterday in a local hospital following a brief illness. Mr. Brown was a native of Nevada City, Calif., born during the early mining days of that region.
>
> For many years, Mr. Brown was engaged in ranching and agricultural work in the Fresno district. He resided at his home on East Belmont Avenue. He was a member of the Catholic Church.
>
> He is survived by a sister, Miss Margaret Brown of Fresno, and several nieces and nephews in the San Francisco and Santa Rosa areas.
>
> The remains will be forwarded by the White's Chapel to San Francisco for funeral services and interment in the family plot at Holy Cross Cemetery, Colma.

**Access:**
- **Fresno County Public Library** - Heritage Hub
- **Newspapers.com** - Fresno Bee archives (1951)
- **California Digital Newspaper Collection**

**Key Information Extracted:**
- Death date: January 7, 1951
- Age: 90 (confirms 1860 birth)
- 50 years in Fresno (arrived c. 1901)
- Residence: East Belmont Avenue, Fresno
- Occupation: Rancher, agricultural worker
- Survivors: Sister Margaret Brown (Fresno), nieces/nephews (SF and Santa Rosa)
- Burial: Holy Cross Cemetery, Colma (family plot)
- Funeral home: White's Chapel, Fresno

## 2. Bridget Joyce Brown (c. 1835-1912)

### Obituary

**Title:** BROWN—[Death Notice]

**Published:** The San Francisco Call, May 23, 1912

**Full Text:**
> BROWN—In this city, May 22, 1912, Bridget, beloved wife of the late Michael Brown, and loving mother of Mary, Catherine, Thomas J., John F., Michael Jr., Margaret, James, and Annie Brown. A native of County Galway, Ireland.
>
> Friends and acquaintances are respectfully invited to attend the funeral tomorrow (Friday), at 9:30 o'clock a.m., from her late residence, 3474 Eighteenth Street; thence to Mission Dolores Church, where a solemn mass of requiem will be celebrated for the repose of her soul.
>
> Interment, Holy Cross Cemetery.
