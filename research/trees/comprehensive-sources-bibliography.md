### United States Census Records

- **Date:** July 12, 1870
- **Members:** Michael Brown (40, miner, Ireland), Bridget Brown (35, keeping house, Ireland), children Mary (13), Catherine (11), Thomas (10), John (7), Michael Jr. (4), Margaret (1)
- **Location:** Nevada City, Nevada County, California
- **Significance:** "Golden record" with complete family unit

- **Household:** Michael Brown and Bridget (Joyce) Brown on Boulder Street

## Clerical & Parish Records

### Episcopal Church Records

**Journal of the Missionary District of Utah (1896)**
- **Event:** Founding and dedication of St. Peter's Episcopal Church, Salt Lake City
- **Location:** 4th South & 2nd West, Salt Lake City, Utah
- **Date:** November 1896
- **Clergy:** Rev. F. J. Vincent named as Priest-in-Charge
- **Significance:** Primary source for church foundation
- **Archive:** Episcopal Church Archives, Austin, Texas

**Journal of the Convocation of Northern Texas (1894)**
- **Event:** "Letter Dimissory" (transfer papers) for Rev. Vincent
- **Transfer:** From Texas missions to Utah
- **Location:** Good Shepherd Mission, Wichita Falls, Texas
- **Significance:** Documents Vincent's "Two-Year Foundation" pattern

**Journal of the Diocese of New York (1888)**
- **Position:** Missionary-at-Large, Hudson Valley Mission chapels
- **Significance:** Vincent's East Coast ministry period
- **Child born:** Frederick Augustus Vincent Jr. (1888, New York)

**Crockford's Clerical Directory (International)**
- **Coverage:** Anglican and Episcopal clergy worldwide
- **Subject:** Frederick Janvrin Vincent
- **Detail:** Tracks ordination, moves between England, New Zealand, and U.S. dioceses
- **Significance:** Professional genealogy of ministry career

**The Living Church Annual (1885-1910)**
- **Publisher:** Episcopal Church
- **Content:** Yearly publication listing clergy assignments
- **Coverage:** Frederick's specific parish assignments:
  - Louisiana (c. 1885) - Good Shepherd Mission, Lake Charles
  - New York (1887-1889) - Missionary-at-Large
  - Texas (c. 1893) - Good Shepherd Mission, Wichita Falls
  - Utah (1894-1896) - St. Peter's Episcopal, Salt Lake City
  - California (1899-1920) - Trinity and St. Stephen's, San Francisco

## Master Bibliography

### Primary Archives

**National Archives and Records Administration (NARA)**
- **Location:** Multiple locations (San Bruno, CA; Washington, DC)
- **Holdings:**
  - San Francisco Passenger Lists (M1410)
  - U.S. Federal Census microfilm (T9, M593, etc.)
  - Immigration records
- **Website:** archives.gov
- **Local:** National Archives at San Francisco (1000 Commodore Drive, San Bruno, CA)

**Episcopal Church Archives**
- **Location:** Austin, Texas
- **Holdings:**
  - Original missionary reports (Utah, Texas districts)
  - Diocesan journals (1880s-1920s)
  - Clerical correspondence
- **Contact:** Archives of the Episcopal Church, 606 Rathervue Place, Austin, TX 78705

**New Zealand National Archives**
- **Locations:** Wellington (main), Christchurch (regional)
- **Holdings:**
  - Tregeagle and Vincent marriage and business records
  - Passenger lists (*Waitangi*, 1876)
  - Anglican church registers
- **Website:** archives.govt.nz

**National Library of Ireland**
- **Location:** Dublin, Ireland
- **Holdings:**
  - Parish registers (Catholic and Church of Ireland)
  - Griffith's Valuation
  - Census fragments (1821-1851)
- **Website:** nli.ie
- **Online:** IrishGenealogy.ie (free parish registers)

**Galway Family History Society**
- **Focus:** County Galway genealogy
- **Special:** "Joyce Country" expertise
- **Purpose:** Tracing Bridget Joyce's specific parish origins
- **Website:** galwayfamilyhistorysociety.org

**Jersey Heritage Trust**
- **Location:** St. Helier, Jersey, Channel Islands
- **Holdings:**
  - Maritime records (Janvrin family)
  - Parish registers (St. Brelade)
  - Property records
- **Website:** jerseyheritage.org
