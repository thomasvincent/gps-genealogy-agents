'''Archer Lyman Durham''' was born on June 9, 1932, in Los Angeles County, California. He grew up in Pasadena, California, and graduated from Pasadena High School.

Durham served in the United States Air Force from January 1953 to 1989, rising to the rank of Major General. His commands included the 1606th Air Base Wing at Kirtland AFB, the 436th Military Airlift Wing at Dover AFB, and the 76th Military Airlift Division at Andrews AFB. His final military position was Director of Deployment at United States Transportation Command.

After retiring from the Air Force, Durham served as Assistant Secretary of Energy for Human Resources and Administration during the Clinton administration.
