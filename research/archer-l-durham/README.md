## Career Timeline

### Military (1953-c.1988)
- **1953**: Aviation cadet
- **1954**: Commissioned, pilot wings (Laredo AFB, TX)
- **1954-1966**: Various assignments (Charleston, Okinawa, Philippines, Utah, France)
- **1966-1975**: Staff positions (Scott AFB, Pentagon, Korea)
- **1975-1977**: Base Commander, McGuire AFB, NJ
- **1977-1979**: Wing Commander, 1606th ABW, Kirtland AFB, NM
- **1979-1980**: Wing Commander, 436th MAW, Dover AFB, DE (only all-C-5 wing)
- **1980-1982**: Division Commander, 76th MAD, Andrews AFB, MD
- **1982-1984**: Vice Commander, Military Traffic Management Command
- **1984-1987**: J-5/IG at USREADCOM; Director of Deployment at JDA
- **1987+**: Director of Deployment, U.S. Transportation Command

### Civilian (c.1993-1997)
- **Assistant Secretary of Energy for Human Resources and Administration**
- Acting head of Quality Management, DOE
